title: "The Costs of Silence: How the Enforcement of Misinformation Regulation Shapes Finfluencers and the Corporate Information Environment"

excerpt: 'ABSTRACT: Social media has reshaped the corporate information environment, but its net effect remains unsettled because of concerns about misinformation. We exploit a 2023 Chinese regulatory crackdown on misinformation by social media finfluencers to examine how enforcement of misinformation regulations affects finfluencers and the corporate information environment. Consistent with Chinese officials facing asymmetric payoff functions that penalize poor outcomes more heavily than they reward favorable outcomes, we find that enforcement increased deletion of finfluencer accounts, particularly among accounts with more negative tones. We further document a chilling effect: following the crackdown, finfluencers whose accounts were not deleted posted fewer negative and positive messages. Finally, we find that enforcement reduced stock price informativeness and stock liquidity. Overall, the evidence suggests that, despite its stated objective, enforcement of misinformation regulations worsened rather than improved the corporate information environment because of officials’ distorted incentives and finfluencers’ strategic responses.
